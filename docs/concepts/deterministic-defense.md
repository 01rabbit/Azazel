# Deterministic Defense

Back to: [System Overview](system-overview.md) | Related: [Azazel-Edge Gateway](../products/azazel-edge.md)

Azazel prioritizes deterministic controls before probabilistic assistants.

## Rationale

- Predictable actions under incident pressure
- Easier auditability and post-incident analysis
- Reduced dependence on remote inference availability

## Practical Implication

AI may assist interpretation and operator workflow, but primary enforcement decisions should remain explicit, bounded, and reproducible.
