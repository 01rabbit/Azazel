# Deception and Delay

Back to: [System Overview](system-overview.md) | Related: [Cyber Scapegoat Gateway](../philosophy/cyber-scapegoat-gateway.md)

Azazel combines deception and delay as coordinated controls.

- Deception changes attacker perception and pathing.
- Delay reduces attacker tempo and forces additional exposure.

Together, they increase observability windows and improve defender decision quality.
